Voting questions and answers

Q: Can I look at my online ballot while in the voting booth?
  • A: Some states do not allow pictures or video at the polling location, or even phones all together. Check if you are in one of those states and bring a paper with your choices instead.
Q: Do I have to bring my ID?
  • A: It depends which state you are in, but most states require a formal ID to vote.
Q: What if I can't make it to the polls on election day?
  • A: Mail-in ballots and early voting can be an option, check with your local government's resources to find out how.

Voting Tips

  • Remember to check our site to find your polling location and times before election day!
  • Using a sample ballot in the polling booth can help you save time.
  • Your vote counts!
